Welcome to HealSpace

Don't know where to begin your exercise journey?

Ever felt like maintaining the healthy lifestyle is difficult each passing day?

Then you've come to the right website. HealSpace has an AI coach
that uses knowledge and information on human psychology and exercise
to imitate that of a personal, human trainer. HealSpace AI provides
exercise activities advice based on your physical disabilities or
disorders, and dietary plans without sacrificing deliciousness and
considering your allergens. During times of demotivation, consult
HealSpace AI to get back up again and gain your passion and fuel back.


Exercise

It's a hastle to think the most efficient activity
to lose calories, especially if you can't see the
progress. Don't you feel like all the time you used
for exercising turn to waste due to lack of results?
Well, HealSpace will help you record the data
of the exercise! Additionally, it'll give you
advice on what activities to do based on your
goals and physical disabilities or disorders.


Input your starting goals to HealSpace AI and any physical disabilities or disorders you have.
The AI then gives you a set amount of exercises you can do, including how much times it should be done.
Once you've completed the activities, make sure to respond to the AI so that it can record
the amount of calories you've burned based on the activity and sets you've done.
Rather than recording steps, HealSpace AI records the meters/kilometers you've completed.

Dietary Plans

Food is secretly an addiction
if we don't recognize it in time.
Unhealthy junk food with tans and
saturated fats inside certain oils.
This is even more prominent and found
in fried and baked foods with butter.
This isn't even considering the allergens
that trigger your allergies, whether mild
or severe outbreak.


After considering potential physical disabilities and getting advice
of different exercise activites you can perform, the AI can now
talk of what healthy ingredients or restaurants that are available to you.
Give HealSpace AI your allergens, preferences, and current food
at your disposal, and it will formulate different recipes for
you to cook and try out. Additionally, if given access to your GPS
and location, HealSpace AI can detect and locate any healthy
restaurants near you. Once again, HealSpace wants to make eating healthy
delicious and safe moment by considering your preferences and allergens.
